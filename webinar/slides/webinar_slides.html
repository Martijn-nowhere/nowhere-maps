MECHANISM 1 / 3

System Thinking

Every module teaches the full chain — not just 'recycling is good' but what happens at every stage.

Where does plastic come from?  ·  How is it sorted?  ·  What actually gets recycled?  ·  What happens when it doesn't?

Kids learn to see the whole picture — not just the end result.

The full-chain approach is what makes the understanding stick. It's not a fact — it's a mental model.
MECHANISM 2 / 3

Real-World Grounding

We don't teach with hypotheticals. We teach with things your child already touches.
